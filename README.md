## 👋 Who I am

I'm an **AI Automation Architect** based in Paris. I build self-healing workflows and autonomous AI agents that eliminate manual bottlenecks — so your team can focus on work that actually matters.

> *"I don't connect apps. I build systems that think, self-correct, and scale."*

**Real results I've delivered:**
- 🏥 **Bastide Confort Médical** — 100% autonomous daily SMS campaign (n8n + CRM API)
- 🚗 **BAATMOON** — Dynamic pricing engine for VTC platform (Python, surge pricing, airport rates)
- 🏭 **B&C Enterprise** — Site-level B2B enrichment for 270 industrial sites (Python + Google Maps API)
- 🏥 **Medical clinic (Upwork ⭐5.0)** — AI PDF extraction of medical prescriptions (n8n + Claude)
